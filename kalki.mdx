Plot Summary
Set in a post-apocalyptic world in the year 2898 AD, the film follows a group of individuals on a mission to save the unborn child of lab subject SUM-80, who is destined to be Kalki, the 10th and final avatar of Lord Vishnu. The story intertwines elements of Hindu mythology with futuristic sci-fi, creating a unique narrative that explores themes of redemption, sacrifice, and the eternal battle between good and evil12.
